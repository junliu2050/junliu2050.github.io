Biography (my CV)

I was born in Yongzhou, Hunan, China and grew up in a small farming village. After I got my B.Sc. degree in Guangzhou, China, I worked as a software engineer for three years and then went back to graduate school in Guangzhou to get my M.S. degree. I obtained my Ph.D. from Southern Illinois University Carbondale under the advisement of Dr. Mingqing Xiao. Since August 2015, I work as a tenure-track assistant professor of Mathematics at Jackson State University in Mississippi. I enjoy spending time with my students and on doing research that benefits our society.

Developed Codes

MATLAB code for 1D Multigrid algorithm

Wed, Jan 4, 2017

Multigrid (MG) methods belong to the best known algorithms for solving some class of PDEs.This code gives a MATLAB implementation of 1D Multigrid algorithm for solving a two-point ODE boundary value problem. Compared with existing online codes, my code tries to balance well between efficiency and readability (simplicity) for practical and wide use.
