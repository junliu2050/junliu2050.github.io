Biography I was born in Yongzhou, Hunan, China and grew up in a small farming village. After I got my B.Sc. degree in Guangzhou, China, I worked as a software engineer for three years and then went back to graduate school in Guangzhou to get my M.S. degree. I obtained my Ph.D. from Southern Illinois University Carbondale under the advisement of Dr. Mingqing Xiao. Since August 2015, I work as a tenure-track assistant professor of Mathematics at Jackson State University in Mississippi.
